About Us.

Established in 1965, Iavazzo's Pizza is a family-owned restaurant. We have been proudly serving Rhode Islanders for over 50 years by providing New York style brick oven pizza and authentic Italian dishes. Made daily with the freshest ingredients.

Open 7 days a week from 11am - 11pm. Whether you're looking to dine-in, take-out, or get delivery, our food is made fresh-to-order every time.
